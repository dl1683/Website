My Projects.

None of these are class projects. None of the class projects, in my opinion, were very useful in learning real skills. Some of these projects involve propietery work and thus I will not be uploading videos of their functions. The rest will be uploaded on my YouTube channel eventually.

This was my most comprehensive project under Dr. Abhyankar. We used the online database as a model to build our own database and run experiments on it. I was responsible for running different directed and undirected models to test out the nature of the data. I also wrote cost functions to determine the more important dimensions of the data.
In the end , we created a method to map the multidimensional input data onto a single binary output set. This gave us a 94% accuracy while not being resource intensive. The algorithm was then formalized as a patent, which has now been monetized. View the terms.

My second major project under Dr. Abhyankar. Done over my last term with him. We were attempting to recreate Biometric information based on partial information. I was working on recreating fingerprints based on minutae points(click to read more)

An attempt to combat foodwaste. Users add their bought items to the app. When the expiry data is approaching, the app alerts the user. Users can ping their location so that delivery people can pick it up. Currently, I am working on adding the scanning functionality for items. Talks to figure out the delivery and distribution are in progress. Currently we are talking to various NGOs to work out the details.

We built an app that took a holistic approach to health and development. I wrote the information extraction protocol that extracted newfeeds from various news organizations and allowed users to customize their news feed based on sources. I also implemented the APIs to create the search, contact and (Gmail) authentication. Our app won the Singapore finals, and was among the 16 (from over 200) called to HongKong to present at the FirstCodeAcademy headquaters for the finals.
